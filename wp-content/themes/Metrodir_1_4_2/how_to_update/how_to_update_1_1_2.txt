How to update Metrodir v.1.1.1 -> v.1.1.2 [Don't forget to back up your database/files before proceeding]
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
--------------------------------- FTP---------------------------------------

- Unzip metrodir.zip to wp-content\themes\metrodir\ and replace files:

	/css/responsive.css
	/include/php/companymarkers.php
	/scripts/ajax_search.js
	/scripts/map.js
	/skl/plugin/user-frontend/css/ufe.css
	/chk-version.php
	/page-search.php
	/style.css

- Go to Metrodir CP at Admin panel and click "save all changes"
